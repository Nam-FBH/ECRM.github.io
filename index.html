Bugak: Gim, Curry Gim, Kelp
이미지
Bugak is a luxurious snack and is considered a representative handmade snack of Korean cuisine.

Gim Bugak is a traditional nutritious snack with a crispy texture made by coating gim(seaweed) grown in clean waters with a thin layer of glutinous rice paste, drying it, and then frying it.
Curry Gim Bugak is made by spreading a thin layer of glutinous rice paste on gim(seaweed) grown in clean waters, drying it, and then deep-frying it. Curry seasoning is added to it to bring out its flavor.
Kelp Bugak is a traditional nutritious snack with a crispy texture made by coating kelp grown in clean waters with a thin layer of glutinous rice paste, drying it, and then frying it.
